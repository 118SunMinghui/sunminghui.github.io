I am an undergraduate student majoring in Applied Physics at Changchun University of Science and Technology. I have conducted research at the Joint International Research Laboratory of Nanophotonics and Biophotonics, where my work is currently under review at an international journal. I later carried out theoretical research at the State Key Laboratory of Solid-State Laser Technology and Applications, where I initiated an independent research project. My research has been supported by the National Key Undergraduate Innovation Training Program.

I am interested in building reliable, physics-informed models for mechanical structure design, with a particular focus on PINNs. I enjoy violin 🎻, mechanical structures 🐙, and handmade art 🎨.
